Giving at The Nest Church

Your generosity helps us do more. Support our mission and ministry through your giving.

Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship and obedience to God. When we give, we participate in God's work and demonstrate our trust in His provision. Your generous contributions enable us to spread the Gospel, support our community, and advance God's kingdom.

Cheerful Giving

"God loves a cheerful giver" - 2 Corinthians 9:7

Faithful Stewardship

We are stewards of God's resources, managing them wisely for His glory.

Kingdom Impact

Your giving supports local ministry and global missions.

Types of Giving

Tithe

Tithing is giving 10% of your income as an act of worship and obedience to God. It's a biblical principle that demonstrates our trust in God's provision and acknowledges that everything we have comes from Him.

"Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this," says the Lord Almighty, "and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it." - Malachi 3:10

Offering

Offerings are gifts given beyond the tithe to support the church's ministry and mission. These voluntary contributions help fund special projects, community outreach, and various ministry initiatives.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." - 2 Corinthians 9:7

Seed Offering

Seed offerings are special gifts given in faith for specific blessings or breakthroughs. Like a farmer who plants seeds expecting a harvest, we give seed offerings trusting God for His provision and blessing.

"Give, and it will be given to you. A good measure, pressed down, shaken together and running over, will be poured into your lap." - Luke 6:38
